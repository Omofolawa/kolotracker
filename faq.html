FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions


How do I change my password?

To change your password, go to the "Change Password" section and enter your old password along with the new password you want to set. Then click on the "Change Password" button.

Can I customize my notification settings?

Yes, you can customize your notification preferences in the "Notification Settings" section. Click on the link provided to access the notification settings.

Where can I find the app version?

You can find the current version of the app in the "App Version" section. It displays the version number of the app.

What are my legal responsibilities?

By using this app, you agree to abide by our Terms and Conditions. You can find more information about your legal responsibilities in the "Legal Responsibilities" section.

Where can I review the privacy notice?

You can review our Privacy Notice in the "Privacy Notice" section. Click on the link provided to access the privacy policy.
